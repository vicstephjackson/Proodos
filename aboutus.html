About Us

Próodos is small 'startup' company born out of a coding bootcamp at the University of California, Riverside. The Próodos name comes the Greek work "πρόοδος", which means "Progress". Progress is what we strive for here at Próodos. The Próodos founders are Mesay Beyene, Victoria Stephenson, and Daniel Jackson.

Our sole purpose is to simplify the way we manage our task. We want to take away the clutter and the sometimes overwelming feeling that we all have to deal with in our lives from time to time. We are in our infancy stages but we are hungry and motivated to grow fast!
